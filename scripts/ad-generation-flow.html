AI Ad Generation — End-to-End Process

URL-to-Campaign flow, CRM → Dev-Goads, published to Meta. As executed for polluxa.com.

Platform: Polluxa CRM Ads (Dev-Goads)  ·  Ad account: act_773958358563901  ·  Target channel: Meta

1

Entry via CRM → Automated Ads

In Polluxa CRM, Marketing → Automated Ads triggers a signed SSO handoff into the Dev-Goads ads app, carrying the workspace identity and live Meta / Google ad credentials in the token.

GET /agency/automated-ads POST /api/auth/crm-login
▼
2

Configure goals & channels

On the Campaign Generator: set placement channel to Meta, daily budget, objective and target regions. Coming-soon channels (TikTok, LinkedIn) are disabled.

Channel: Meta Budget: $/day
▼
3

Add product — crawl & AI analysis

Paste the product URL (polluxa.com). The backend crawls the site, then an LLM extracts a grounded product profile (name, category, value proposition, features).

POST /api/onboarding/scrape POST /api/onboarding/analyze-product Fix: body limit 100kb → 10mb
▼
4

Generate strategy & creatives (AI agents)

A background worker runs the research + agent pipeline: strategy, audience, creative, offer and objection-handling agents produce a full campaign with multiple grounded ad variants (headlines, primary text, CTAs).

POST /api/campaigns/generate 16 variants generated
▼
5

Review the draft campaign

The app routes to the campaign view — status draft, per-variant copy shown, spend/impressions at zero. Nothing has touched the ad network yet.

GET /api/campaigns/:id Status: draft
▼
6

Launch to Meta (published PAUSED)

Launch builds the real Meta object graph via the Graph API — ad set → ad creative → ad per variant on account act_773958358563901. Every object is created PAUSED by design: real objects, zero spend until explicitly activated.

POST /api/campaigns/:id/launch graph.facebook.com/v22.0 7 Meta variants → PAUSED Google → failed (invalid customer id / 401)
▼
7

Activate & optimize (optional, spends)

A paused variant can be flipped live with Activate (this begins real delivery and spend). Once live, metrics ingest on a schedule and the optimization engine reallocates budget toward winners.

POST /api/campaigns/:id/variants/:vid/activate Metrics → optimize loop
